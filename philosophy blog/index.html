Who is Avel Dumanskyy?

Avel Dumanskyy started as a fictional philosopher character by the mind of Nico Behrens, an American creative writer and poet. Now, they have grown to be a philosophical persona to separate Behrens' creative works with their philosophical, rhetorical ones. Though, they may appear in fictional works occasionally on sebatical.

What are Dumanskyy's Credentials?

They are to graduate Lindenwood University in May of 2026 with a BA in Philosophy & Religion. They have also spent part of their high school career studying ethics and logic under a specialized homeschool program.

What is this blog for?

This blog is meant to be a place to record thoughts on certain philosophical topics. This can range from preliminary inquries to full considerations with philosophical-historical background. Think of this blog as a place of reflection or a personal journal. Most if not all posts will not be peer-reviewed.
